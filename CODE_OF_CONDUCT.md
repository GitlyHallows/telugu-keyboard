# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Telugu Keyboard to be a respectful project for Telugu speakers, developers, testers, and first-time contributors. We expect participants to communicate with patience, clarity, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different Telugu dialects, spelling habits, and typing habits.
- Discuss corrections with examples and evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the issue or pull request.
- Help new contributors understand the project standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Dismissing dialect or usage differences without discussion.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer.
